    5 Tips for Perfecting Your Grilled Chicken
    1. Choose the Right Cut: Start with boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs for even cooking and easy marinating.
    2. Marinate for Flavor: Marinating adds depth to the taste. Opt for a blend of olive oil, herbs, and your favorite seasonings.
